# Expense Reimbursement Standard Operating Procedure

**Department:** HR / Finance  
**Classification:** Internal  
**Version:** v1 | **Last Updated:** 2026-01  
**Owner:** People Operations  
**Access:** HR team

---

## Overview

This SOP defines the process for employees to submit, approve, and receive reimbursement for business expenses. It covers allowable expense categories, spending limits, submission deadlines, and the approval workflow in Workday.

All reimbursements are processed on the last working day of each month for claims submitted by the **20th of that month**.

---

## Allowable Expense Categories

### Travel

| Expense Type | Policy |
|-------------|--------|
| Flights | Economy class for flights <6 hours. Business class approved for 6+ hour flights with manager pre-approval. |
| Train / Bus | Actual cost, standard class |
| Taxi / Rideshare | Actual cost. Must include destination and business purpose. |
| Personal vehicle | $0.67/km (IRS mileage rate 2026) |
| Rental car | Economy class. Collision insurance included in corporate card policy. |
| Accommodation | Up to $250/night in standard cities; $350/night in high-cost cities (NYC, SF, London, Singapore). |
| Meals (traveling) | Up to $75/day, itemized |
| Parking / Tolls | Actual cost |

### Client Entertainment

- Pre-approval required for expenses >$150 per person
- Must document: attendees, company, business purpose
- Alcohol included in meal limit (not separately reimbursable beyond the meal cap)
- Max $200/person for client meals without VP approval

### Home Office (Remote Employees)

One-time home office setup: up to $500 (desk, chair, monitor)
Monthly internet subsidy: $50 (no receipt required — submitted as a fixed allowance)

### Professional Development

- Books / online courses: up to $200/year without pre-approval
- Conferences: requires manager approval before purchase
- Training / certifications: covered by L&D budget (separate process — see Learning & Development Policy)

## Non-Reimbursable Expenses

The following will NOT be reimbursed:

- Personal expenses incurred alongside business travel (room upgrades, minibar, pay-per-view)
- Fines, penalties, traffic violations
- Expenses submitted more than **60 days** after incurred
- Expenses without a receipt (exception: per-diem meals ≤$15 and items <$25)
- Expenses that exceed policy limits without pre-approval
- First-class flights or luxury hotel upgrades without executive approval

## Submission Process

### Step 1 — Gather receipts

All expenses >$25 require an itemized receipt. Acceptable formats:
- Original paper receipt (scanned)
- PDF email receipt
- Screenshot of in-app receipt (Uber, Lyft, etc.)

Credit card statements alone are **not** sufficient — itemized receipts are required.

### Step 2 — Submit in Workday

1. Log in to Workday > **Expenses > Create Expense Report**
2. Set the report title: `[Month Year] - [Your Name] - [Trip/Purpose]`
3. Add each expense line item:
   - Category (select from dropdown)
   - Date
   - Amount and currency
   - Business purpose (mandatory)
   - Receipt (upload)
4. For travel expenses: attach the trip itinerary or meeting confirmation
5. Click **Submit**

### Step 3 — Approval workflow

| Amount | Approvers |
|--------|----------|
| < $500 | Direct manager |
| $500 – $2,000 | Direct manager + Finance |
| > $2,000 | Direct manager + Finance + VP |

SLA: approvals within 5 business days. Finance processes approved reports in the next monthly cycle.

---

## Foreign Currency Expenses

- Submit in the original currency paid
- Workday will convert using the exchange rate on the transaction date
- Attach bank/credit card statement showing the conversion if disputing the rate

---

## Corporate Card Holders

Employees with a corporate card must:
1. Submit receipts in Workday within 30 days of the transaction date
2. Reconcile all card statements monthly
3. Disputed charges: contact Finance within 10 business days of statement date

Personal use of the corporate card is grounds for immediate card cancellation and potential disciplinary action.

---

## Late or Amended Submissions

- Expenses submitted 31–60 days after the incurred date require manager and Finance sign-off
- Expenses submitted >60 days after the incurred date will not be reimbursed (no exceptions)
- To amend a submitted report: contact Finance at `finance-ops@sentinelcorp.internal`

---

## Escalation

If your reimbursement is not processed within **10 business days** of approval, contact:
1. Your HR Business Partner
2. Finance team: `finance-ops@sentinelcorp.internal`
3. If unresolved after 5 more business days, escalate to HR Director
