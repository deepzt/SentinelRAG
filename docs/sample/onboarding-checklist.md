# New Hire Onboarding Checklist

**Department:** HR  
**Classification:** Internal  
**Version:** v1 | **Last Updated:** 2026-03  
**Owner:** People Operations  
**Access:** HR team

---

## Overview

This checklist guides the HR team and hiring managers through the complete new hire onboarding process, from offer acceptance to Day 30 check-in. Each section identifies who is responsible for completing the items.

New hires are assigned a **buddy** from their team on Day 1. Buddies are not responsible for compliance tasks — they focus on culture and social integration.

---

## Pre-Start (HR + IT)

**Responsible: HR Coordinator + IT**

### Week Before Start Date

- [ ] Confirm start date and working location (remote / office / hybrid) with new hire
- [ ] Send welcome email with Day 1 logistics (time, location, point of contact, dress code)
- [ ] Confirm legal right-to-work documentation received and verified
- [ ] Signed employment contract on file in HRIS (Workday)
- [ ] Payroll details submitted to Finance (bank account, tax form)
- [ ] IT account provisioning:
  - [ ] Company email created (`firstname.lastname@sentinelcorp.com`)
  - [ ] Slack workspace invite sent
  - [ ] GitHub organization invite sent (engineering roles)
  - [ ] Google Workspace access provisioned
  - [ ] JIRA / Confluence access granted
  - [ ] VPN credentials issued
- [ ] Hardware ordered and scheduled for delivery (laptop, peripherals)
- [ ] Building access card prepared (for office-based employees)
- [ ] 30/60/90-day goals template sent to hiring manager

---

## Day 1 — Welcome

**Responsible: HR + Hiring Manager + Buddy**

### Morning

- [ ] HR welcome meeting (30 min): company overview, values, org chart
- [ ] Complete mandatory compliance training in LMS (Workday Learning):
  - [ ] Information Security Awareness
  - [ ] Code of Conduct
  - [ ] Anti-Harassment and Discrimination Policy
  - [ ] Data Privacy (GDPR/CCPA overview)
- [ ] Confirm all IT access is working (email, Slack, GitHub, JIRA)
- [ ] Hardware setup complete and verified
- [ ] Tour of office / virtual office Slack channels

### Afternoon

- [ ] Hiring manager 1:1 (60 min): role expectations, team structure, immediate priorities
- [ ] Team introduction meeting
- [ ] Buddy introduction and first social chat (coffee / virtual)
- [ ] New hire reads and signs off on:
  - [ ] Employee Handbook acknowledgement
  - [ ] NDA / Confidentiality Agreement
  - [ ] Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)
  - [ ] IP Assignment Agreement

---

## Week 1 — Systems and Context

**Responsible: Hiring Manager + Team**

- [ ] Enroll in benefits (health, dental, vision, retirement) — deadline: 10 business days from start
- [ ] Complete payroll setup in Workday (withholding, direct deposit)
- [ ] Attend company all-hands (next scheduled one)
- [ ] Shadow 3+ team meetings this week
- [ ] Read team wiki / Confluence pages relevant to role
- [ ] Set up 1:1s with key stakeholders (manager, cross-functional peers)
- [ ] Engineering-specific:
  - [ ] Clone and run the main service locally
  - [ ] Complete dev environment setup doc
  - [ ] First PR: fix a `good-first-issue` ticket
  - [ ] Pair-program with a team member for at least 2 hours
- [ ] HR-specific:
  - [ ] Access HRIS (Workday) and familiarize with employee records
  - [ ] Shadow payroll processing for the current cycle
  - [ ] Meet HR Business Partners for each department

---

## Week 2–4 — Integration

**Responsible: Hiring Manager**

- [ ] Weekly 1:1s with manager established (recurring calendar invite)
- [ ] Quarterly goals and OKRs set in Workday Performance
- [ ] 30-day check-in scheduled with HR
- [ ] Training plan for first 90 days agreed with manager
- [ ] Access to all required internal systems confirmed
- [ ] Expense reimbursement process explained (see Reimbursement SOP)
- [ ] Leave and PTO process explained (see Leave Policy 2026)
- [ ] Emergency contact and dependant details submitted to HR
- [ ] T-shirt / swag size and mailing address collected (remote employees)

---

## Day 30 Check-in

**Responsible: HR Business Partner**

Conduct a 30-minute 1:1 with the new hire to cover:

- [ ] Confirm onboarding experience was positive — note any issues
- [ ] Verify all compliance training completed (LMS report pulled)
- [ ] Benefits enrollment confirmed
- [ ] Any concerns about role, team, or integration? Document if yes
- [ ] Benefits enrollment deadline has passed — confirm no outstanding items
- [ ] Update onboarding checklist status in HRIS to "Complete"
