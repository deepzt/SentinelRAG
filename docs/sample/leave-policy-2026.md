## Overview

This policy defines all categories of employee leave, eligibility criteria, accrual rates, and the application process. This document is confidential and for internal HR use only. Employees should refer to the Employee Handbook (available on the intranet) for their applicable leave summary.

Questions regarding leave entitlements should be directed to `hr@sentinelcorp.internal`.

---

## Leave Categories

### Annual Leave (PTO)

Annual leave accrues on a calendar year basis beginning from the employee's start date.

| Employment Band | Annual Entitlement | Accrual Rate |
|-----------------|-------------------|--------------|
| IC1 – IC3       | 15 days           | 1.25 days/month |
| IC4 – IC6       | 20 days           | 1.67 days/month |
| Manager+        | 25 days           | 2.08 days/month |
| Executive       | 30 days           | 2.5 days/month  |

**Key rules:**
- Maximum carryover: 10 days into the next calendar year. Excess is forfeited on January 31.
- PTO cannot be taken in advance of accrual (no negative balance).
- A minimum of 5 consecutive days PTO must be taken before December 31 each year.
- Requests must be submitted at least **5 business days** in advance for leaves ≤ 5 days.
- Requests of **6+ days** require **2 weeks' notice**.

---

### Sick Leave

All employees receive 12 sick days per year, non-accruing, refreshed January 1.

- Sick leave does not carry over and has no cash value.
- For absences of **3+ consecutive days**, a medical certificate is required and must be submitted to HR within 5 business days of return.
- Sick leave can be used for the employee's own illness or to care for an immediate family member (child, spouse/partner, parent).
- Employees on probation accrue sick leave at the same rate as permanent staff.

---

### Parental Leave

#### Primary Caregiver Leave

- **16 weeks** fully paid parental leave for the primary caregiver.
- Applicable to biological, adoptive, and foster parents.
- Must be taken within 12 months of birth, adoption finalization, or placement date.
- Can be taken as a continuous block or in minimum 2-week segments.
- Notice required: **8 weeks** before expected leave start.

#### Secondary Caregiver Leave

- **4 weeks** fully paid leave for the secondary caregiver.
- Must be taken within 6 months of birth or placement.
- Notice required: **4 weeks** before expected leave start.

---

### Bereavement Leave

| Relationship | Paid Leave |
|-------------|-----------|
| Spouse / Partner | 5 days |
| Child | 5 days |
| Parent / Sibling | 3 days |
| Grandparent / In-law | 2 days |
| Close friend / Extended family | 1 day (manager discretion) |

Additional unpaid leave may be requested. HR will evaluate case-by-case.

---

### Jury Duty and Legal Obligations

Employees called for jury service will receive full pay for the duration of service, up to **15 working days**. Beyond 15 days, the employee receives the difference between jury pay and their base salary.

Employees must notify their manager and HR as soon as they receive the jury summons and provide a copy of the official notice.

---

### Unpaid Leave of Absence

Unpaid leave may be granted at management discretion for:
- Extended personal circumstances not covered by other leave types
- Study leave for professional development
- Voluntary work (sabbaticals)

**Eligibility:** Minimum 1 year tenure.  
**Maximum duration:** 3 months. Extensions reviewed individually.  
**Process:** Request via HRIS > Leave > Leave of Absence form. Requires line manager and HR Business Partner approval.

---

## Leave Application Process

### Submitting a Leave Request

1. Log in to **Workday** (`workday.sentinelcorp.internal`)
2. Navigate to **Time Off > Request**
3. Select leave type and dates
4. Add a note for your manager if relevant
5. Submit — your manager receives an automated approval request

### Approval Workflow

- **PTO (≤ 5 days):** Line manager approval required (SLA: 2 business days)
- **PTO (>5 days):** Line manager + HR Business Partner approval (SLA: 5 business days)
- **Parental / Bereavement / Sick:** Auto-approved with retrospective documentation

### Emergency / Unplanned Leave

If leave is unplanned (e.g., sudden illness):
1. Notify your manager directly (phone/Slack) **before the start of your working day**
2. Submit the Workday request retrospectively on the day of return
3. HR should be CC'd for any unplanned absence of 3+ consecutive days

---

## Public Holidays

The company observes all public holidays in the employee's country of employment as listed in the **Public Holiday Calendar** on the intranet. Employees required to work on a public holiday receive a substitute day off within 30 days.

---

## Policy Violations

Misrepresentation of leave reasons, failure to follow the application process, or unauthorized absence may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination. All leave records are auditable by HR.
